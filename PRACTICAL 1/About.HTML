Welcome to Ramanujan College


Ramanujan College is a trend-setter amongst the colleges of the University of Delhi. The College provides a perfect blend of tradition and modern approach to education. Equipped with the best of facilities the college environment is enriched with a spirit of bonding and a sense of commitment.

The college offers a high level of teaching – learning experience that is complimented by a wide range of co-curricular activities. The various departments, college societies, centres and cells help the students to enhance skills and make wise choices in the future to become exemplary successful humans.

I urge you to make the most of the innumerable opportunities for self-development and betterment of the society that are offered by this vibrant institution. Success in life comes to those who optimize one’s potential and the college will support you in your endeavours. Build your skills, learn new things and follow a sustainable and ethical way of life because that will bring out the best in you. Make the optimum use of the facilities available while maintaining a fine balance between academic and cocurricular activities. Enjoy the stay at Ramanujan College.
